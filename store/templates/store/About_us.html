About Us

At Omi Multipurpose, we are committed to transforming the way farm products reach Nepali households. Our mission is simple yet powerful — to deliver farm-fresh, organic, and pesticide-free goods from local sellers directly to buyers in a timely and transparent manner. We work closely with trusted farmers and producers who cultivate their crops using sustainable practices, free from harmful chemicals and preservatives.

Our platform is a bridge that connects conscious consumers with ethical producers. We offer a wide range of essential farm products including unrefined oils, organically grown rice, seasonal vegetables, and much more — all carefully curated to ensure top quality and freshness. Every item sold on our site is sourced with care and delivered with a promise of purity and health.

In a time where food safety and nutrition are more important than ever, we aim to empower both buyers and sellers by creating a reliable online marketplace that values trust, health, and community. Join us in our journey to build a healthier Nepal — one meal, one farm, and one honest trade at a time.
